A Snippet About Myself

I'm bubbly, sassy, and motivated. I'm easy to talk with, very approachable, and very open. People often say I'm very energetic and strong-minded - qualities that make me, me. As a female wanting to go into tech, I've learned to grow tough skin and to stand up for what I believe in. With that being said, I'm motivated. When I set my eyes on something, I really go for it. I'm willing to sacrifice time and energy to get what I want. In addition, I love to joke around when time is appropriate and I love to laugh. I would say my cure for everything is laughter. I also love conversing with people about different opinions and events that go on in the world, exposing myself to new thoughts and perspectives.

Sports

The first time I was ever part of a basketball team was in 4th grade. I was horrible at first, but as I grew up, basketball grew up with me. Now, I love basketball and it's become a part of me. I participated in my schools' teams since the fifth grade, something I am very proud of. I was able to be the JV captain when I was a sophomore at my high school and last year, I was selected to be part of the varsity team. Basketball has taught me so many things about character and life, helping shape me to become the person I am today.

Since the 8th grade, I have admired Kevin Durant, who still remains my favorite player today. It's crazy how many facts that I know about him but what can I say? I'm a fan! (Go Warriors!!)

Apart from basketball, I like watching baseball and sprints.

Academics

Since I could remember, I've loved math. It's so fun solving problems. I love how one problem can have a single answer but so many ways to approach it.

I tutor people for math occasionally and it's been a humbling experience. It feels awesome when you see a student's face light up when he or she understands a concept.

One of the most inspirational and influential people in my life is my math teacher, Mr. Peralta, who has allowed me to explore my passion for math on a deeper level.
